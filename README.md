#  Learnify — AI-Powered Quiz & Leaderboard App

  A smart, competitive quiz platform powered by **Gemini AI** — where your knowledge is your rank.

##  Overview

**Learnify** is a web application that generates personalized quizzes on any topic using Google's Gemini API. Users choose a topic 
and difficulty level, answer AI-generated questions, and earn scores based on their performance. All scores feed into a 
**global leaderboard**, so players can compete with each other in real time.

##  Features

-  AI-Generated Questions** — Powered by Gemini API; fresh questions generated every session
-  Topic of Your Choice** — Enter any subject you want to be quizzed on
-  Difficulty Preferences** — Choose from Easy, Medium, or Hard before you begin
-  Performance Scoring** — Score calculated based on the number of correct answers
-  Global Leaderboard** — Compete with other users and climb the rankings
-  User Authentication** — Create an account to track your scores and join the competition

##  How It Works

1. Sign Up / Log In** — Create your account to get started
2. Choose a Topic** — Enter any subject you'd like to be quizzed on
3. Set Difficulty** — Pick Easy, Medium, or Hard based on your confidence
4. Answer Questions** — Gemini AI generates a questionnaire tailored to your input
5. Get Your Score** — Results are calculated from your correct answers
6. Check the Leaderboard** — See how you rank against all other players
